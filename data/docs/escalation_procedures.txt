ESCALATION PROCEDURES

Level 1: General Support
- Response time: 4 hours
- Handles: Account questions, billing inquiries, basic technical issues
- Contact: support@example.com

Level 2: Technical Team
- Response time: 8 hours  
- Handles: DNS issues, email problems, SSL certificates, domain transfers
- Escalate when: Technical issue requires system access or configuration

Level 3: Abuse Team
- Response time: 24 hours
- Handles: Spam reports, phishing complaints, malware, security issues
- Escalate when: Domain involved in abuse, legal complaints, security threats

Level 4: Billing Department
- Response time: 4 hours
- Handles: Payment issues, refunds, billing disputes, invoice questions
- Contact: billing@example.com

When to Escalate:
- Customer requests manager or supervisor
- Issue unresolved after 2 attempts
- Requires system-level access
- Legal or compliance matter
- Potential security threat
