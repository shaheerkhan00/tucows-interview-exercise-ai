WHOIS INFORMATION REQUIREMENTS

ICANN Requirements:
All domain registrants must provide accurate WHOIS information as mandated by ICANN policy. Failure to maintain accurate WHOIS can result in domain suspension.

Required Information:
1. Registrant Name (individual or organization)
2. Registrant Email (must be monitored)
3. Registrant Phone (must be reachable)
4. Registrant Address (complete mailing address)
5. Administrative Contact (can be same as registrant)
6. Technical Contact (can be same as registrant)

Privacy Protection:
- WHOIS privacy service available for eligible TLDs
- Hides personal information from public WHOIS
- Costs $5/year per domain
- Not available for .us, .ca, and some country codes

Updating WHOIS:
- Log into account portal
- Navigate to Domain Management > WHOIS
- Update required fields
- Changes take effect within 24 hours
- You will receive verification email

Verification Process:
- Email verification required within 15 days
- Click link in verification email
- Failure to verify results in suspension
- Re-send verification from domain management

Common Issues:
- Email bouncing: Update to valid email address
- Phone invalid: Must include country code and be reachable
- Address incomplete: Include street, city, state/province, postal code, country
