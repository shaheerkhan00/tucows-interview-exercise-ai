TECHNICAL SUPPORT GUIDE

DNS Configuration:
- DNS changes can take 24-48 hours to propagate globally
- Use our nameservers: ns1.example.com and ns2.example.com
- Common DNS records: A (IPv4), AAAA (IPv6), CNAME (alias), MX (mail), TXT (verification)

Email Setup:
- Configure email clients with:
  - Incoming: mail.yourdomain.com (IMAP port 993, POP3 port 995)
  - Outgoing: smtp.yourdomain.com (port 587 with STARTTLS)
- Use your full email address as username

SSL Certificates:
- Free SSL certificates available for all domains
- Auto-renewal enabled by default
- Installation takes 10-15 minutes
- Verify at https://www.ssllabs.com/ssltest/

Domain Transfers:
- Unlock your domain in the control panel
- Obtain authorization (EPP) code
- Initiate transfer at new registrar
- Approve transfer email within 5 days
- Transfer completes in 5-7 days

Common Issues:
- Site not loading: Check DNS settings and nameservers
- Email not working: Verify MX records and email client settings
- SSL errors: Ensure certificate is installed and not expired
